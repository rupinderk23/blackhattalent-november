Aayana Dance Company
Where Tradition Meets Innovation
Aayana Dance Company is a rising star in the world of performing arts, earning rave reviews wherever they perform. Known for their seamless fusion of Indian classical and contemporary dance styles, this hidden gem has been mesmerizing audiences with performances that blend spirituality, storytelling, and modern creativity. Each choreography offers a transformative experience, reimagining age-old traditions with innovative movements and emotive expression. Whether on grand stages or intimate gatherings, Aayana's artistry embodies love, life, and light, leaving a lasting impression and inspiring a deeper connection to India’s cultural heritage.

Revant Himatsingka
The Voice of Clean Eating and Transparency
Revant Himatsingka, popularly known as "Food Pharmer" on Instagram, is a dynamic content creator and nutrition advocate, and a compelling speaker on nutrition and mindful consumption. A graduate of NYU Stern School of Business, Revant left behind a promising career in finance and an MBA offer from IIM Bangalore to champion the cause of clean eating. Known for his bold stance against FMCG giants, he highlights hidden sugars and misleading claims in packaged foods, sparking important conversations on transparency in the food industry. Author of the book Selfienomics and a sought-after speaker at TEDx and educational institutions, Revant combines humor and insight to inspire mindful consumption and empower India’s youth to make healthier choices.

A Culinary Journey for BMW Excellence

Chef Garima Arora

The BMW Excellence Club recently hosted Freude, an exclusive two-day celebration of joy, craftsmanship, and innovation in the heart of Delhi. The event embodied the spirit of Freude – German for "joy" – while spotlighting the pinnacle of sustainable luxury with the all-electric BMW i7.

A key highlight of the evening was the exceptional culinary experience presented by Chef Garima Arora, the first female Indian chef to earn two Michelin stars. For this event, she and her team flew in from Bangkok to create a seven-course meal that perfectly complemented the theme of sustainability, aligning with the launch of the all-electric BMW i7. Her approach emphasized zero-waste practices and showcased how humble ingredients and everyday recipes can be elevated to evoke boundless joy and surprise.

The event was a true blend of innovation and indulgence, leaving BMW’s patrons inspired by the future of mobility and the art of living well.

Palo Alto's Exclusive CXO Event in Vizag

VVS Laxman & Nashpreet Singh

Adil Manuel Collective

Palo Alto recently hosted an exclusive R&R event with networking for partner CXOs, bringing together 130 distinguished guests for an evening that seamlessly blended inspiration and entertainment.

The highlight of the evening was a talk session by VVS Laxman, one of cricket's most respected figures, skillfully moderated by sports anchor Nashpreet Singh. Known for his legendary partnership with Rahul Dravid in the 2001 Eden Gardens Test, Laxman shared his insights on leadership, pressure management, and the importance of staying current with trends. He discussed his experiences in high-pressure situations, the evolution of cricket, and his approach to nurturing raw talent.

Following the session, the evening transitioned into a vibrant networking event, featuring a performance by the Adil Manuel Collective. Known for their eclectic mix of jazz, blues, and soul, the band energized the room with their dynamic sound, providing the perfect backdrop for networking

The Asian Paints New Product Launch Celebration Continues

Kathak Rockers

Fashion show in Bangalore

Fashion Show in Mumbai

Fashion Show in Kolkatta

Golden Girls

Following the success of its revamped product line launch in Chennai, Hyderabad, and Bangalore, Asian Paints continued to captivate its audience with two more stunning events in Mumbai and Kolkata. Dealers and clients were treated to an extraordinary blend of culture, creativity, and innovation, further amplifying the excitement around the brand's refreshed offerings.

In all five cities, our agency produced a stunning fashion show on short notice, seamlessly assembling models, choreography, costumes, makeup, and backstage arrangements. Directed by renowned choreographer Vahbiz Mehta the shows added depth and elegance to the evening’s experience.

In Mumbai, the evening was further enlivened by the Kathak Rockers , who delivered a mesmerizing fusion performance. Their act incorporated the high-energy Marathi hit 'Zingat', adding a regional flavor that delighted the audience.

In Kolkata, the grandeur reached new heights with the Golden Girls, a group of 26 dancers, who took the stage with an elaborate Bollywood fusion performance customized for Asian Paints. A highlight of their act was a creative tribute to the brand, as they formed the names Nilaya, Ultima, and Royale—representing Asian Paints' product ranges—using intricate hand formations. Their closing act saw the dancers holding placards that displayed the event tagline 'Reimagine Colour & Decor', leaving the audience inspired by their vibrant and thoughtful presentation.
